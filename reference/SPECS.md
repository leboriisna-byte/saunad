# Tesler 3.3m Barrel Sauna — Technical Specifications

## Source: Product page + Blueprint drawing

### Overall Dimensions
- **Total length**: 3300mm
- **Barrel diameter**: 2050mm (radius 1025mm)
- **Wall thickness**: 40mm spruce
- **Support base width**: 1500mm

### Internal Layout (2 rooms)
- **Changing room (eesruum)**: 950mm long (front section)
- **Sauna room (leiliruum)**: 1780mm long (back section)
- **Dividing wall** separates the two rooms

### Benches (leili lava)
- **Material**: Alder (lepp)
- **Width**: 500mm each side
- **Height from floor**: 450mm
- **Position**: Both sides of sauna room

### Front Wall (with overhang/nokk)
- The front has an extended overhang ("nokk" = beak) — the barrel extends beyond the front wall
- **Vertical planks** on the end wall
- **Wooden door** with multi-pane glass window, lockable
- Door has handle on left side

### Back Wall
- **Vertical planks** on the end wall
- **Small window** (bronze-tinted glass for sauna room)
- **Chimney pipe** exits through the roof near the back

### Roof
- **Icopal Plano** hexagonal/honeycomb bitumen shingles
- Covers the top ~120° arc of the barrel
- Dark gray/black color
- **Chimney** — stainless steel pipe through roof

### Barrel Body (staves)
- **Horizontal planks** running lengthwise along the barrel
- Individual stave lines visible
- Light pine/spruce color (natural or oiled)
- **Finish**: Remmers HK Lasur or Tikkurila terrace oil

### Support Legs
- **V-shaped/angled** wooden cradle supports
- 2 supports (at ~200mm from each end based on blueprint)
- Darker thermo-treated wood

### Heater Options
- **Wood-burning stove** (puuküttekeris) — positioned against dividing wall in sauna room
- **Electric heater** (elektrikeris) — alternative
- Stones on top of stove
- **Chimney** through roof for wood-burning option

### Other Features
- **Stainless steel bands** with adjustable clamps around barrel
- **Ventilation system** — circular vent hole visible on front wall
- **Water tank**: 30 or 40 liters (customer choice)
- **Floor grates** (põrandarestid) in both rooms
- No building permit required
